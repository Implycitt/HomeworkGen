			Vocabulary
	1. Blackboard: a sheet of smooth, hard material, especially dark slate, used in schools, lecture rooms, etc., for writing or drawing on with chalk.
	2. Himself: an emphatic appositive of him  or he1: He himself spoke to the men.
	3. Reporter: a person who reports. 
	4. Board: a piece of wood sawed thin, and of considerable length and breadth compared with the thickness.
	5. Indulge	: to yield to an inclination or desire; allow oneself to follow one's will (often followed by in): Dessert came, but I didn't indulge. They indulged in unbelievable shopping sprees.
	6. Ring: a typically circular band of metal or other durable material, especially one of gold or other precious metal, often set with gems, for wearing on the finger as an ornament, a token of betrothal or marriage, etc.
	7. Bookworm: a person devoted to reading or studying.
	8. Inflatable: capable of being inflated. 
	9. Salesclerk: a person who sells goods in a store.
	10. Butterscotch: a flavor produced in puddings, frostings, ice cream, etc., by combining brown sugar, vanilla extract, and butter with other ingredients.
	11. Inimical: adverse in tendency or effect; unfavorable; harmful: a climate inimical to health.
	12. Snapshot: an informal photograph, especially one taken quickly by a handheld camera.
	13. Camera: a device for capturing a photographic image or recording a video, using film or digital memory.
	14. Interim: an intervening time; interval; meantime: School doesn't start till September, but he's taking a Spanish class in the interim.
	15. Shellfish: an aquatic animal having a shell, as the oyster and other mollusks and the lobster and other crustaceans.
	16. Campus: the grounds, often including the buildings, of a college, university, or school.
	17. Invest: to put (money) to use, by purchase or expenditure, in something offering potential profitable returns, as interest, income, or appreciation in value.
	18. Ship: a vessel, especially a large oceangoing one propelled by sails or engines.
	19. Catfish: any of the numerous fishes of the order or suborder Nematognathi (or Siluroidei), characterized by barbels around the mouth and the absence of scales.
	20. Jackpo: the chief prize or the cumulative stakes in a game or contest, as in bingo, a quiz contest, or a slot machine.
